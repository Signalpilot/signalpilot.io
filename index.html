ACCUMULATION
Follow the money. Cumulative delta reveals hidden accumulation.

The ribbon shows cumulative buying vs selling:

  • • Green Ribbon: Buyers have control—cumulative buying pressure dominates
  • • Red Ribbon: Sellers have control—cumulative selling pressure dominates
  • • Centerline Crosses: Dots appear when flow crosses zero—regime shift in progress
  • • White Dots: Extreme accumulation or distribution levels reached
  • • Divergence Marks: Price hits new highs/lows but flow doesn't confirm—hidden weakness
