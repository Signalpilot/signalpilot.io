SP — Pentarch

★ FLAGSHIP
Complete trend regime detection system. Not just tops and bottoms—the full market cycle.

Maps complete market reversal sequences across 5 distinct phases:

  • • 🟢 TD (Touchdown): Detects potential bottoming formation—fires when selling pressure shows exhaustion after downtrend
  • • 🟢 IGN (Ignition): Signals bullish structure break—appears when price reclaims key levels with momentum after TD
  • • 🟡 WRN (Warning): Early weakness detection in uptrends—fires when internal conditions start deteriorating, first sign distribution may be starting
  • • 🔴 CAP (Capitulation): Exhaustion thrust detector—triggers on extreme volume spikes with price extensions, classic blow-off top pattern
  • • 🔴 OUT (Breakdown): Signals bearish structure break—appears when price loses key support with conviction, marks topping completion

Scaling: Works on all timeframes (1 minute to yearly)
Markets: Stocks, crypto, forex, indices, commodities, futures—anything with OHLCV data
Regime: Built-in bull/bear/neutral detection using 3-factor voting

Most oscillators just say "overbought" or "oversold." Pentarch shows you WHERE you are in the complete 5-phase reversal sequence. When you see TD → IGN, you're watching a potential bottom unfold. When you see WRN → CAP → OUT, you're watching a potential top form in real-time.

SP — Plutus Flow

ACCUMULATION
Follow the money. Cumulative delta reveals hidden accumulation.

The ribbon shows cumulative buying vs selling:

  • • Green Ribbon: Buyers have control—cumulative buying pressure dominates
  • • Red Ribbon: Sellers have control—cumulative selling pressure dominates
  • • Centerline Crosses: Dots appear when flow crosses zero—regime shift in progress
  • • White Dots: Extreme accumulation or distribution levels reached
  • • Divergence Marks: Price hits new highs/lows but flow doesn't confirm—hidden weakness

Price can fake a move. Volume can't. When the ribbon turns green and starts climbing, buyers are building pressure. When price hits a new high but the ribbon stays flat, you get the divergence signal—something's broken.

HTF levels, D/W/M pivots, VWAP, VAL/POC—all key zones in one view.

Shows you every institutional reference point automatically:

SP — Harmonic Oscillator

TIMING
Perfect entry timing through phase‑aligned momentum synthesis.

Four momentum oscillators vote on every bar:

  • • Component Voting: Each of 4 oscillators votes Bull / Bear / Neutral on every bar
  • • Live Vote Count: Table displays current voting breakdown in real-time
  • • Multi-Confirmation: Only signals when multiple oscillators agree on direction
  • • Star Rating System: ★ to ★★★★ shows confidence level (4/4 agreement = max stars)
  • • Composite Line: Main indicator combines all four momentum readings into single view

One oscillator can be wrong. Four agreeing is different. When the table shows 4/4 Bulls and you get ★★★★ stars, everything's aligned. When it's 2 Bulls vs 2 Bears, they're fighting—no trade.

Signal Pilot is a suite of seven non-repainting TradingView indicators built for trend regime detection, smart money flow analysis, and multi-timeframe confluence. The flagship indicator, Pentarch™, maps complete market cycles with five precision events.

Zero repainting. Guaranteed. All signals finalize on candle close. We audit every indicator for lookahead bias. If you can prove any repaint, we pay you $100 USD. What you see in history is exactly what you would have seen live.

No. Signal Pilot is an educational toolset only. We provide technical analysis tools—not investment advice, trade recommendations, or guaranteed returns. You are solely responsible for your trading decisions.
