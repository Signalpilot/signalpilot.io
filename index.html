What’s inside the Trading Suite

SP‑EC Pro — Event‑Candle Confluence

Flagship
Signals, not guesses — bias in one look.

Shows when the market likely wants up or down—clean signals that don’t repaint.

Non‑repainting engine combining consensus from EMA34/55, price vs Pilot Line, and slope. ATR‑normalized dip/rip logic, Pilot ribbon for trend strength, NanoFlow “+” for micro‑momentum. Alert‑ready.

SP‑PVA Volume + SP‑PVA Overlay

Crown energy, measured — then printed.

Confirms participation and prints Bull/Bear plus Climax or Absorption spikes.

Normalized participation (log Z‑Vol + RVOL) with HTF confirm, CVD, divergences, and spike detection. Overlay emits one decisive non‑repainting print: Bull / Bear + EVR (Climax / Absorption). TF‑aware & scan‑friendly.

SP‑OBV — Money flow

Pressure flips, you catch the hint.

Reveals where volume leans; crosses hint when buyers or sellers take control.

OBV with smoothing basis and Bollinger channel. Crosses/band pushes flag pressure shifts. Regular + hidden divergences; optional HTF compute; alerts included.

SP‑LTF — Rhythm & fatigue

Coiled or cooked — at a glance.

Marks when price is building energy or getting tired so you don’t chase late moves.

TD‑style 8/9 counts, Squeeze cloud for coil, and Caution/Danger “X” for overextended moves (fade, mean‑stretch, squeeze releases).

SP‑Levels — Micro reference

Levels that hold the story.

Auto‑plots day/week/month anchors price actually respects—without clutter.

dVWAP, wVWAP, dPOC, wOpen, mOpen, mPOC—drawn on the opening candle only. Sharp references; minimal noise.

SP‑RSI Triad — Layered RSI

Momentum turns — with receipts.

Blends fast/base/slow RSI to spot turns early and scores their strength.

Multi‑speed RSI with divergence strength score (0–50). Filters: OBV trend, Stoch RSI timing, ATR vol, HTF RSI. Clear visual guides.

SP‑SRSI+ — Context‑aware Stoch RSI

Context first, trigger second.

Adds trend, volume, and volatility context so signals make sense.

Volatility‑adaptive logic that respects HTF bias, listens to volume/money flow, stays aware of SDZ; emits a unified Confluence Score for scans/alerts.

SP‑MACD+ — Momentum regime

Classic MACD, grown up.

Cuts chop and confirms regime so you ride trends, not noise.

HTF confirmation, ATR gating, near‑zero fade, optional pre‑smooth to reduce chop. Modes: Raw or stricter Qualified. Presets: Classic Soft / Pro Filtered.

SP‑SDZ — Supply/Demand zones

Supply or demand — no ambiguity.

Boxes true supply (red) and demand (green) so your context is crystal clear.

Confirms swing highs/lows, boxes the real zone (wick + body), and projects forward—minimal noise; maximum structure.

SP‑Screener — On‑chart & global

Scan the list, not your patience.

Check many symbols/timeframes at once and fire alerts automatically.

On‑chart mini‑screener: up to 8 symbols × TFs; shows ticker, last signal (↑/↓), time since, TP1 (ATR‑sized), Gauss Ribbon state, running P&L. Global/watchlist alerts supported.

How it works — from bias to execution

1) Set bias
Use SP‑EC Pro and/or SP‑MACD+ for directional regime. Don’t trade against it.
2) Verify participation
Confirm with PVA Overlay (Bull/Bear/EVR) and OBV/CVD. No energy = no trade.
3) Map structure
Respect SDZ supply/demand boxes and Levels (d/w/m anchors) for clean context.
4) Time entries
RSI Triad & SRSI+ for turns with receipts. LTF flags “coiled vs cooked”.
5) Scale & alert
Route signals via SP‑Screener (on‑chart/global). Keep risk fixed, not feelings.
Built‑in plays
  • Trend Buy‑the‑Dip: Bias Up → PVA Bull/Climax → reaction near demand/dVWAP → RSI Triad/SRSI+ timing.
  • Reversal Flip: Absorption + OBV divergence at supply → EC slope flattens → Pilot reclaim.
  • Breakout Continuation: Climax through wVWAP/supply → first ATR dip → NanoFlow “+”.

You don’t need every module on every chart. Start with EC Pro (bias) + PVA/OBV (participation) + SDZ/Levels (structure); layer the rest as needed. Educational only — not financial advice.
