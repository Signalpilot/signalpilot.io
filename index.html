How it works — from bias to execution

1) Set bias
Use EC Pro and/or MACD+ to pick a side. Don’t trade against it.
2) Verify participation
PVA/OBV confirm energy. No energy = no trade.
3) Map structure
Respect SDZ boxes and Levels (d/w/m anchors).
4) Time the entry
RSI Triad & SRSI+ for turns; LTF flags coil vs fatigue.
5) Automate
Screener routes signals to alerts. Keep risk fixed, not feelings.
Built‑in plays
  • Trend Buy‑the‑Dip: Bias Up → PVA Bull/Climax → reaction near demand/VWAP → RSI/SRSI timing.
  • Reversal Flip: Absorption + OBV div at supply → EC slope flattens → Pilot reclaim.
  • Breakout Continuation: Climax through wVWAP/supply → first ATR dip → NanoFlow “+”.

Start with EC Pro (bias) + PVA/OBV (participation) + SDZ/Levels (structure). Layer timing & screener as needed. Educational only — not financial advice.
