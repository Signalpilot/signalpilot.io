What’s inside the Trading Suite

SP‑EC Pro — Event‑Candle Confluence

Your bias in one glance. Signals, not guesses.

Shows when the market likely wants up or down—clean signals that don’t repaint.

Consensus from EMA34/55, price vs Pilot Line and slope. ATR‑normalized dip/rip logic, Pilot ribbon for trend strength, and NanoFlow “+” for micro‑momentum. Non‑repainting and alert‑ready.

SP‑PVA Volume + SP‑PVA Overlay

Crowd energy, measured — then printed.

Confirms participation and prints Bull/Bear plus Climax or Absorption spikes.

Normalized participation (log Z‑Vol + RVOL) with HTF confirm, CVD and divergence checks. Overlay emits one decisive, non‑repainting print: Bull / Bear + EVR (Climax / Absorption). Scan‑friendly.

SP‑OBV — Money flow

When pressure flips, you see it.

Reveals where volume leans; crosses hint when buyers or sellers take control.

Smoothed OBV with a Bollinger channel. Crosses and band pushes flag shifts in control. Regular + hidden divergences, optional HTF compute, and alert presets.

SP‑LTF — Rhythm & fatigue

Coiled or cooked—know before you chase.

Marks when price is building energy or getting tired so you don’t chase late moves.

TD‑style 8/9 counts, squeeze cloud for coil, and Caution/Danger marks for stretched moves (fade, mean‑revert, post‑squeeze).

SP‑Levels — Micro reference

Anchors that actually hold the story.

Auto‑plots day/week/month anchors price respects—without clutter.

dVWAP, wVWAP, dPOC, wOpen, mOpen, mPOC—drawn on the opening candle only. Sharp references; minimal noise.

SP‑RSI Triad — Layered RSI

Momentum turns—with receipts.

Blends fast/base/slow RSI to spot turns early and scores their strength.

Fast/Base/Slow RSI blend with a 0–50 strength score. Filters: OBV trend, Stoch timing, ATR vol, HTF RSI.

SP‑SRSI+ — Context‑aware Stoch RSI

Trigger only when context agrees.

Adds trend, volume, and volatility context so signals make sense.

Volatility‑adaptive Stoch RSI that respects HTF bias, money‑flow and SDZ; outputs a single Confluence Score for scanning and alerts.

SP‑MACD+ — Momentum regime

Classic MACD—minus the chop.

Cuts chop and confirms regime so you ride trends, not noise.

HTF confirm, ATR gating and near‑zero fade reduce noise. Modes: Raw or Qualified. Presets: Classic Soft / Pro Filtered.

SP‑Screener — On‑chart & global

Scan the list, not your patience.

Check many symbols/timeframes at once and fire alerts automatically.

On‑chart mini‑screener: up to 8 symbols × TFs; shows ticker, last signal (↑/↓), time since, ATR‑sized TP1, Ribbon state, and running P&L. Global/watchlist alerts supported.
