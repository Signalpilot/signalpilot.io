FAQ

🎯 What is Signal Pilot?

Signal Pilot is a suite of seven non-repainting TradingView indicators built for trend regime detection, smart money flow analysis, and multi-timeframe confluence. The flagship indicator, Pentarch™, maps complete market cycles with five precision events.

🔄 Do the signals repaint?

Zero repainting. Guaranteed. All signals finalize on candle close. We audit every indicator for lookahead bias. If you can prove any repaint, we pay you $100 USD. What you see in history is exactly what you would have seen live.

💼 Is this financial advice?

No. Signal Pilot is an educational toolset only. We provide technical analysis tools—not investment advice, trade recommendations, or guaranteed returns. You are solely responsible for your trading decisions.

📊 What markets & timeframes work?

Anything on TradingView: crypto, forex, stocks, indices, commodities, futures. Works on all timeframes from 1-minute scalps to weekly swings. Each indicator is calibrated per timeframe for optimal performance.

💻 Does it work on free TradingView?

Yes! You just need enough indicator slots for your setup. Free accounts get 3 slots, which is enough for Pentarch + 1-2 filters. Pro/Premium accounts get more slots and unlimited alerts.

⏱️ How fast is activation?

PayPal/Card: 1–8 hours (usually under 2 hours). Crypto: 1–3 hours after blockchain confirmation. You'll receive TradingView invite-only access via email. Check your TradingView notifications.

💎 What's included in my plan?

Every plan includes: All 7 indicators (The Elite Seven), all future updates, ongoing support, documentation, presets, and access to new indicators as they launch. Lifetime includes everything, forever.

🔐 How many devices can I use?

One TradingView username per license. You can sign in on unlimited devices with that username. Account sharing is prohibited—each subscription is tied to one TradingView account.

₿ Do crypto payments auto-renew?

No. Crypto payments are one-time prepaid passes (30/90/365 days or Lifetime). No auto-renewal. You get a 20% discount for paying with crypto (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, USDT, USDC).

💳 Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel via email (support@signalpilot.io) or PayPal. No penalty. You keep access until the end of your current billing period. No partial refunds after 30 days—see refund policy below.

🛡️ What's your refund policy?

30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within 30 days of your first payment, we refund you in full—no questions asked. After 30 days, cancellation ends future billing but no refund. See full refund policy.

💬 How do I get help?

Email support@signalpilot.io for any questions, issues, or feedback. We respond within 24 hours (usually much faster). Check docs.signalpilot.io first for instant answers.
