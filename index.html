SP — Pentarch

★ FLAGSHIP
Complete trend regime detection system. Not just isolated signals—the full market cycle.

Think of Pentarch like a traffic light for the market — it shows you when the market is showing strength (green), weakness (red), or transitioning between the two (yellow).

🎯 The Five Key Moments Pentarch Spots

  • • 🟢 TD (Touchdown): "Downward momentum may be exhausting" — Price has fallen for a while, downside pressure is weakening
  • • 🟢 IGN (Ignition): "Upward momentum is building" — Price is breaking up and upward pressure is strengthening
  • • 🟡 WRN (Warning): "Something's not right" — Price is still rising but showing early signs of weakness
  • • 🔴 CAP (Climax): "Upward momentum may be exhausting" — Price pushed too far too fast, upside pressure is weakening
  • • 🔴 BDN (Breakdown): "Downward momentum is building" — Price is breaking down and downward pressure is strengthening

📍 The Pilot Line (Your Trend Guide)

  • • Green line = Trend is up — indicates bullish momentum
  • • Red line = Trend is down — indicates bearish momentum
  • • Orange line = Trend is transitioning — neither bulls nor bears have clear control

🎨 Candle Colors (Quick Visual Guide)

  • • Green candles = Market structure is bullish
  • • Red candles = Market structure is bearish
  • • Makes it easy to see at a glance what the overall market condition is

⚡ NanoFlow Crosses (Momentum Check)

  • • Little green crosses = Upward momentum is healthy
  • • Little red crosses = Downward momentum is healthy
  • • Lots of crosses = Strong trending move. No crosses = Weakening trend

How to read it: Look at the candle colors to see the overall market structure → Check the Pilot Line to see the trend direction → Watch for the 5 event signals to know when something important is happening → Use NanoFlow crosses to gauge the strength of the move.

Works on: Any market (stocks, crypto, forex, futures) and any timeframe (from 1-minute charts to daily/weekly charts)

Bottom line: Pentarch shows you where you are in the market cycle. Green signals (TD → IGN) = early cycle phase. Yellow/Red signals (WRN → CAP → BDN) = late cycle phase.

SP — Plutus Flow

ACCUMULATION
Cumulative delta tracking shows demand/supply pressure over time.

Think of Plutus Flow like a tug-of-war meter — it keeps score of whether demand or supply pressure is winning the battle over time.

  • • Green Ribbon: Demand is winning — cumulative upward pressure is higher than downward pressure
  • • Red Ribbon: Supply is winning — cumulative downward pressure is higher than upward pressure
  • • Centerline Crosses (dots): The battle is shifting sides — momentum is changing direction
  • • White Dots: Extreme levels reached — the demand or supply pressure is getting very strong
  • • Divergence Marks: Price and flow disagree — price makes a new high but flow doesn't, which signals hidden weakness

Why it matters: Price can make deceptive moves, but volume flow tells a more honest story. When the ribbon turns green and climbs, it shows consistent demand. When price hits a new high but the ribbon stays flat (divergence), it suggests the move lacks real support.

Bottom line: Plutus Flow tracks the cumulative balance of demand vs supply pressure. It helps you see if a price move has real volume behind it or if it's just noise.

Bottom line: Janus Atlas is like having a roadmap of important price levels. It shows where price might bounce or break, based on what other traders are watching.

SP — Harmonic Oscillator

TIMING
Four oscillators vote on every bar. Multi-confirmation timing system.

Ever had one indicator say "bullish" and another say "bearish"? Harmonic Oscillator solves this by combining 4 different momentum indicators and showing you when they all agree.

  • • 4 Oscillators Vote: Four different momentum indicators each vote "Bullish," "Bearish," or "Neutral" on every candle
  • • Live Vote Count: A table shows the real-time vote breakdown (e.g., "3 Bulls, 1 Neutral")
  • • Multi-Confirmation System: Signals only appear when multiple oscillators agree on the same direction
  • • Star Rating (★ to ★★★★): More stars = more agreement. Four stars means all 4 oscillators agree
  • • Composite Line: One main line that combines all four readings for easy viewing
