How it works — follow the checklist

1
Set your biasSP‑EC Pro / SP‑MACD+
  • Do: Add EC Pro + MACD+.
  • Check: Pilot ribbon color & slope agree with EC prints (R↑/T for up, R↓/B for down).
  • Green light: Bias is obvious in one look.
  • If not: No trade. Wait for alignment.
2
Verify participationPVA Overlay / OBV‑CVD
  • Do: Turn on PVA Overlay + OBV.
  • Check: Overlay prints Bull for longs / Bear for shorts; OBV slope agrees.
  • Green light: Volume backs the move.
  • If not: Stand aside. No fuel, no trade.
3
Pick the locationSDZ + Levels
  • Do: Plot demand/supply zones + d/w VWAP, opens, POCs.
  • Check: Longs near demand / shorts near supply, never mid‑air.
  • Green light: Price reacts at a known level.
  • If not: No location, no entry.
4
Time the triggerRSI‑Triad / SRSI+ / LTF
  • Do: Watch RSI‑Triad score, SRSI+ confluence, LTF squeeze releases.
  • Check: Trigger matches your bias at the level.
  • Green light: Enter on close; place ATR stop behind the structure.
  • If not: Wait for the next clean trigger.
5
Automate & manageScreener / Alerts
  • Do: Send webhook alerts; use Screener to find new A‑setups.
  • Check: Partial TPs laddered; equity guardrails respected.
  • Green light: Journal the trade; review weekly.
  • If not: Pause after a drawdown day. Reset.

FAQ

Does SP‑EC repaint?

No. Event candles finalize on the candle close. Alerts fire on confirmed signals only.

What markets/timeframes?

Any symbol on TradingView, any timeframe. Modules adapt from scalps to swings.

Can I scan a watchlist?

Yes. SP‑Screener supports on‑chart mini scans and global/watchlist alerts.

Do I need every module on every chart?

No—bias (EC/MACD+), participation (PVA/OBV), structure (SDZ/Levels), timing (RSI‑Triad/SRSI+/LTF), discovery (Screener).
