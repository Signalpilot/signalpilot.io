Strategy modules built for real traders

SignalPilot’s TradingView strategy is packaged into modular blocks so you can enable only the logic that fits your playbook: automation, position sizing, and broker-ready alerts.

Trend foundation

Layer moving average clouds, structure swings, and volume bias to pre-filter bad markets before a setup even forms.

  • Multi-timeframe bias overlay with optional Heikin Ashi smoothing.
  • Adaptive higher-high/low tracking to avoid chop.
  • Session filters keep you aligned to liquid trading hours.

Signal sculpting

Combine oscillators, order flow zones, and candle pattern scoring to confirm when the move is worth taking.

  • Weighted confluence scoring exposes confidence at a glance.
  • False break and liquidity sweep filters keep you patient.
  • Alert templates auto-name entries for TradingView pushes.

Execution & management

Automated entries, position sizing, and broker alerts adapt to volatility so risk stays consistent.

  • Dynamic stop placement using ATR and market structure.
  • Partial take-profit ladders with time-in-trade guardrails.
  • Webhook-ready payloads for bots and copy-trading desks.
