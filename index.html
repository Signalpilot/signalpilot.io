How it works — from chart to decisions

  1. Set bias with SP‑EC Pro. If event candles align and the Pilot ribbon is supportive, you have a directional edge.
  2. Check participation with PVA + OBV. Look for PVA Bull/Bear or EVR (Climax/Absorption) and supportive money‑flow.
  3. Map structure with SDZ + Levels. Prefer entries near demand in uptrends and supply in downtrends; use d/w VWAP and opens as anchors.
  4. Time entries with RSI Triad / SRSI+ / LTF. Take the turn when your timing layer agrees (divergence score, K/D turn, fatigue release).
  5. Alert & manage with Screener. Route alerts to watchlists or bots; use the on‑chart mini‑screener for quick multi‑symbol checks.
Three ready‑to‑use playbooks
  • Trend Buy‑the‑Dip: Bias Up + PVA Bull/Climax → reaction at demand/dVWAP → RSI Triad/SRSI+ timing.
  • Reversal Flip: Absorption + OBV divergence at supply → EC slope flattens → Pilot reclaim.
  • Breakout Continuation: Climax through wVWAP/supply → first ATR dip → NanoFlow “+”.
