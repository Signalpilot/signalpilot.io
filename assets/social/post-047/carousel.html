Inducement
Fake moves designed to trigger stop losses and trap traders on the wrong side. The sweep before the move.

Order Blocks
Zones where institutions may have positioned. Usually the last candle before a strong move away.

Price often returns to order blocks before continuing.

Concept 4
Fair Value Gap (FVG)
Imbalances in price caused by rapid moves. Gaps between candles that often get revisited.

Price tends to fill these gaps before continuing.

Confirmation of a direction change. When price breaks a significant high (bullish) or low (bearish).

BOS confirms the shift in market structure.
