The Problem Isn't the Tool
Every indicator was designed for a specific market condition.

Use it in the wrong one and it will fail. Every. Single. Time.

Context is everything.

Common Mismatch #1
RSI Overbought in Trends
"RSI is overbought, time to short!"
In strong trends, overbought stays overbought. You short. It keeps going. You get chopped.

04 / 08
Common Mismatch #2
MA Crossovers in Ranges
"Golden Cross! Time to buy!"
In choppy markets, crossovers produce constant whipsaws. Death by a thousand cuts.

05 / 08
Common Mismatch #3
MACD Without Trend Context
"MACD crossed up, long signal!"
MACD is a lagging momentum indicator. Without knowing if you're in a trend or range, signals are meaningless.
