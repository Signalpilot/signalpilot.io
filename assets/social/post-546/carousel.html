Slide 2 - What Is a Gap
SIGNALPILOT

WHAT IS A GAP?

  • Price opens away from previous close
  • No trading occurred in between
  • Creates visual "gap" on chart
  • Often signals significant event

Slide 3 - Breakaway Gap
SIGNALPILOT

BREAKAWAY GAP

  • Starts a new trend
  • High volume
  • Often doesn't fill quickly
  • Signals major shift in sentiment

Slide 4 - Continuation Gap
SIGNALPILOT

CONTINUATION GAP

  • Middle of a move
  • Confirms trend strength
  • Also called "measuring gap"
  • Projects potential target
04 / 08
Slide 5 - Exhaustion Gap
SIGNALPILOT

EXHAUSTION GAP

  • End of a move
  • Last gasp before reversal
  • Often fills quickly
  • Watch for reversal signals

Slide 6 - The "Gaps Fill" Myth
SIGNALPILOT

THE "GAPS FILL" MYTH

  • Many gaps fill, but not all
  • Breakaway gaps may never fill
  • Context determines probability
  • Don't blindly fade every gap
