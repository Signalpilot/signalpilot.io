WHAT IS A BREAKER?

  • Price breaks previous structure
  • The broken level flips role
  • Old support → new resistance
  • Old resistance → new support

WHY IT WORKS

  • Traders trapped at broken level
  • They exit when price returns
  • Their exits fuel the new direction
  • Supply/demand flip at the zone

BEARISH BREAKER

  • Support holds multiple times
  • Price breaks below support
  • Support becomes resistance
  • Price returns and rejects
SIGNALPILOT
Slide 5 — Bullish Breaker

BULLISH BREAKER

  • Resistance holds multiple times
  • Price breaks above resistance
  • Resistance becomes support
  • Price returns and bounces

HOW TO IDENTIFY

  • Find clear structure break (BOS)
  • Mark the origin of the breaking move
  • Watch for price to return to zone
  • Look for reaction/rejection
