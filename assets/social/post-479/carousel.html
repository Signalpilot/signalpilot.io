THE CONCEPT

  • Mark Asian session high and low
  • These become targets for later sessions
  • London/NY often sweep one side
  • Real move often follows the sweep

WHY IT WORKS

  • Asian range builds liquidity
  • Stops accumulate above and below
  • Active sessions hunt this liquidity
  • Sweep creates entry opportunity

STEP 1: MARK THE RANGE

  • Mark the high
  • Mark the low

STEP 2: WAIT FOR SWEEP

  • London or NY opens
  • Watch for break of Asian high or low
  • Quick wick and reversal = sweep
  • Sustained break = possible trend

STEP 3: LOOK FOR REVERSAL

  • After sweep, look for reversal setup
  • Confirmation from price action
  • Target opposite side of range or beyond
