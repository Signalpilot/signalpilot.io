Definition

What Is a Liquidity Sweep?

Price briefly penetrates a key level to trigger stop losses, then immediately reverses.

Institutions use this to fill large orders at better prices and trap retail traders.

Pattern
Liquidity Sweep
  • Quick wick below support
  • Triggers stop losses
  • Fast reversal back above
  • Traps shorts

Speed is key. Sweeps happen fast.

Real Breakdown

Breakdowns are sustained, not snappy.
