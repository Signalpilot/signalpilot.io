WHAT IS SFP?

  • Price breaks a swing high/low
  • But fails to close beyond it
  • Wick shows the attempt
  • Body shows the rejection

Slide 3 — Bearish SFP
03 / 08

BEARISH SFP (at highs)

  • Price wicks above previous high
  • Closes back below it
  • Suggests exhaustion/trap
  • Potential reversal signal
