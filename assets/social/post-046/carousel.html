NO STOP = EVENTUALLY ZERO

A stop loss isn't defeat. It's defining your risk BEFORE the trade.

02 / 08
Type 1
Fixed Stop
Set percentage or dollar amount from entry.
Simple but not adaptive to market conditions.

Example: 2% of account, $50 max loss per trade.

SIGNALPILOT
03 / 08
Type 2
Structure Stop
Below support for longs, above resistance for shorts.
Based on chart logic, not arbitrary numbers.

Your stop has a reason: the trade thesis is invalid there.

SIGNALPILOT
04 / 08
Type 3
ATR Stop
Based on volatility. Usually 1.5-2x Average True Range.
Adapts to current market conditions.

Wider in volatile markets, tighter in calm markets.

SIGNALPILOT
05 / 08
Type 4
Trailing Stop
Moves with profit. Locks in gains as price moves.
Great for trending markets, tricky in ranges.

Let winners run while protecting profits.
