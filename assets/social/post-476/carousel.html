ACCUMULATION

  • Institutions build positions
  • Done quietly in ranges
  • Absorbs selling pressure
  • Precedes bullish moves

DISTRIBUTION

  • Institutions offload positions
  • Often at market highs
  • Sells into retail buying
  • Precedes bearish moves

SIGNS OF INSTITUTIONAL FLOW

  • Large wicks / sweeps
  • Quick reversals at key levels
  • Volume spikes at extremes
  • Failed breakouts
