WHAT NOT TO DO

  • Revenge trade to "make it back"
  • Increase size to recover faster
  • Skip your process rules
  • Trade from desperation

Slide 3 — What To Do
03 / 07

WHAT TO DO

  • Step back and review objectively
  • Identify if losses were process or execution
  • Accept that drawdowns are normal
  • Return to your system with discipline
SIGNALPILOT
Slide 4 — Size Reduction
04 / 07

SIZE REDUCTION STRATEGY

  • Temporarily reduce position size
  • Rebuild confidence with small wins
  • Focus on process, not P&L
  • Scale back up gradually
SIGNALPILOT
Slide 5 — Process Review
05 / 07

PROCESS REVIEW

  • Journal every trade during the streak
  • Look for pattern of errors
  • Check if market conditions changed
  • Separate bad luck from bad decisions
