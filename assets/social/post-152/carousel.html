BREAKAWAY GAP

Occurs at the start of a new trend, breaking out of a consolidation pattern. Usually accompanied by high volume.

Trading: Trade in direction of gap, rarely fills quickly

Slide 3 — Runaway

RUNAWAY GAP

Appears mid-trend, signaling strong continuation momentum. Also called "measuring gap" as it often marks halfway point.

Trading: Add to positions, trend likely to continue

SIGNALPILOT
Slide 4 — Exhaustion
04 / 06

EXHAUSTION GAP

Occurs at the end of a trend, representing the last push before reversal. Often fills quickly as momentum fades.

Trading: Watch for reversal, consider fading the gap

Slide 5 — Common
05 / 06

COMMON GAP

Happens randomly in trading ranges with no significant news. Low volume and fills within a few days typically.

Trading: Fade toward gap fill, low significance
