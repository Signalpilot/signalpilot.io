WHY IT'S DANGEROUS

  • Creates overconfidence

  • Leads to oversized positions
  • Disappointment when reality differs

BETTER APPROACH

  • Walk forward bar by bar
  • Make decisions with incomplete info
  • Then reveal what happened

  • Don't confuse the two
  • Humility is realistic
