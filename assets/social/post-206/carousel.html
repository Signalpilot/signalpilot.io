Strategy #1

Opening Range Breakout

ORB Strategy

Wait for first 15-30 min range to form

Mark the high and low of that range

Trade breakout above or below the range

Direction often continues for the session

Use range width for stop loss placement

The opening range breakout is one of the oldest and most reliable day trading strategies.
