Hit Daily Loss Limit

You've reached your pre-defined maximum loss for the day. This is your circuit breaker activating.

Action: Close charts. No more trades today. Period.

Why it matters: Chasing losses leads to revenge trading, which leads to bigger losses. Your predetermined limit exists for exactly this reason.

Feeling Emotional

Angry after a loss? Frustrated by missed entries? Fearful of another red candle? These emotions cloud judgment.

Action: Step away. Go for a walk. Clear your head.

The truth: Every impulsive trade made in an emotional state reinforces bad habits. The market will be there tomorrow. Your capital might not be.
