Slide 2 - The Body

THE BODY

  • Distance between open and close
  • Shows who won the session
  • Green/white = buyers won
  • Red/black = sellers won
02 / 08
Slide 3 - The Wicks
SignalPilot

THE WICKS

  • Price that was rejected
  • Upper wick = sellers pushed back
  • Lower wick = buyers pushed back
  • Long wicks = strong rejection
03 / 08
Slide 4 - The Size
SignalPilot

THE SIZE

  • Large body = strong conviction
  • Small body = indecision
  • Long wicks = rejection
  • Size tells the story's intensity
04 / 08
Slide 5 - Hammer
SignalPilot

HAMMER (Long Lower Wick)

  • Sellers tried, buyers overwhelmed
  • Potential reversal signal
  • At bottom of downtrend = bullish
  • Rejection of lower prices
05 / 08
Slide 6 - Doji
SignalPilot

DOJI (Tiny Body, Equal Wicks)

  • Neither side won
  • Complete indecision
  • Potential turning point
  • Watch for next candle confirmation
06 / 08
Slide 7 - Marubozu
SignalPilot

MARUBOZU (Full Body, No Wicks)

  • Complete dominance
  • No rejection at all
  • Strong conviction
  • Trend continuation signal
