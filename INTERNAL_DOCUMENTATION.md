## Products — The Elite Seven

All 7 indicators included in every plan. Non-repainting. All markets. All timeframes.

### 1. SP - Pentarch (Flagship)
5-phase market reversal cycle detection:
- **TD** (Touchdown) → **IGN** (Ignition) → **WRN** (Warning) → **CAP** (Climax) → **BDN** (Breakdown)
- 4 integrated layers: Regime bars, Pilot Line, NanoFlow Crosses, Event Signals

### 2. SP - OmniDeck
All-in-one overlay unifying 10 premium systems:
- TD Sequential, Squeeze Cloud, Bull Market Support Band
- SuperTrend Ensemble, Regime System, Supply/Demand Zones
- Candlestick Patterns, Liquidity Sweeps, EMA Events, Caution/Danger Warnings

### 3. SP - Volume Oracle
Institutional accumulation/distribution tracking:
- Smart money flow detection with strength percentages
- Early warning flashes before regime flips
- Built-in position calculator

### 4. SP - Plutus Flow
Cumulative delta ribbon:
- Green ribbon = buyers control, Red = sellers control
- White dots = extreme accumulation/distribution
- Divergence marks reveal broken trends

### 5. SP - Janus Atlas
Institutional reference points:
- HTF levels (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly)
- VWAP anchors, Volume Profile zones (POC, VAH, VAL)
- Session markers (Asia, London, NY)
- Structure labels (BOS, CHoCH)

### 6. SP - Augury Grid
Multi-symbol watchlist:
- Track 8+ tickers simultaneously
- Live signal direction, time since signal, target prices
- Quality score (0-100) confluence rating
- Running P&L tracker

### 7. SP - Harmonic Oscillator
4-oscillator voting system:
- Star ratings ★ to ★★★★ = agreement level
- Real-time breakdown (e.g., "3 Bulls, 1 Neutral")
- Composite line combines all oscillators
