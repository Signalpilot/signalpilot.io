## TROUBLESHOOTING PROMPTS

### If ads get disapproved:
```
My ad was disapproved for [reason]. Here's the ad copy: [paste copy].
How can I modify it to comply with Google Ads policies while keeping the message?
```

### If Quality Score is low:
```
My keywords have low Quality Score. The keyword is "[keyword]" and my landing page is signalpilot.io.
What can I do to improve relevance between the keyword, ad copy, and landing page?
```

### If CPC is too high:
```
My average CPC for "[keyword]" is $X which is too high.
What alternative keywords or match types could I use to lower costs while maintaining relevance?
```
