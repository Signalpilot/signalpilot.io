## Compliance Notes

### Financial Services Disclaimer
- Google may require financial services verification for EU markets
- US/UK/CA/AU/IN generally have fewer restrictions for trading education tools
- Landing page includes "Educational use only. Not financial advice." disclaimer

### Prohibited Claims to Avoid
- "Guaranteed profits"
- "Make money trading"
- Specific return percentages
- "Get rich" language

### Recommended Messaging
- Focus on education and tools
- Emphasize "educational purposes"
- Highlight the learning/system aspect vs. profit promises
