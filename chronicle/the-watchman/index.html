Quality Scores: The Language of Confluence

The quality score is the heart of Augury Grid. It's not a prediction‚Äîit's a measurement of consensus among The Seven.

High Quality (80%+)

Multiple systems align. Pentarch confirms the cycle phase. Volume Oracle shows institutional positioning. Plutus Flow confirms pressure. The Commander's systems agree. This is a high-conviction setup.

Medium Quality (60-79%)

Some systems agree, others are neutral or conflicting. The setup has merit but lacks full confluence. Trade with reduced size or wait for improvement.

Low Quality (Below 60%)

The signals conflict. Systems point in different directions or show no clear bias. The Watchman's verdict: stand aside. Let others gamble on unclear conditions.

Quality scores change in real-time as conditions evolve. A setup that starts at 70% might build to 90% as more signals align‚Äîor decay to 50% as the opportunity passes.
