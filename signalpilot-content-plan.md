- **Morning:** 9:00 AM EST
- **Evening:** 7:00 PM EST

### Compliance Rules

- No directive language ("you should," "look for," "buy," "sell")
- All content observational and educational
- No financial advice claims
- No guaranteed outcomes
- Language: "tends to," "often," "may indicate," "historically"
