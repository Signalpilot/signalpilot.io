Frequently Asked Questions

Is SignalPilot financial advice?
No. SignalPilot is educational. Always validate in a demo environment before risking capital.
What’s the difference between Lite and Pro?
Lite is a free TradingView indicator for visual bias and confidence. Pro is an invite‑only strategy that adds automation, risk modules, and broker/webhook alerts.
How do I get access to Pro?
Email support@signalpilot.io with subject “SignalPilot Pro Waitlist”. We’ll reach out with onboarding steps when slots open.
Which markets are supported?
Major crypto pairs, forex majors, indices, equities/ETFs, and commodities. See the “Trade the markets you care about” section on the homepage.
Do you store my data?
We keep minimal operational data (e.g., server logs). See Privacy Policy.

Additional questions can be sent to support@signalpilot.io.
