1. Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with Signal Pilot. If our indicators don't meet your expectations, we offer a straightforward refund policy:

  • 7-Day Window: Full refund if you're not happy (first-time purchasers only)
  • No Questions Asked: Just let us know you'd like a refund
  • Fair Process: Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 Who Qualifies?

First-time purchasers of any Signal Pilot plan are eligible for a full refund within 7 days of purchase.

You qualify if:

  • This is your first Signal Pilot purchase
  • You request a refund within 7 calendar days of payment
  • Your account has not been banned for Terms violations

You do NOT qualify if:

  • You previously received a refund from Signal Pilot
  • More than 7 days have passed since payment
  • You purchased during a "no refund" promotional period (rare, clearly disclosed)
  • You violated our Terms of Service (sharing access, fraud, etc.)

2.2 Plan-Specific Rules

Plan Type Refund Window Conditions
Monthly Subscription 7 days Full refund, first-time only
Quarterly Subscription 7 days Full refund, first-time only
Yearly Subscription 7 days Full refund, first-time only
Lifetime Access 7 days Full refund, first-time only (treat as final purchase)

3. How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Contacting Support

Email support@signalpilot.io with:

  • Subject: "Refund Request"
  • Your TradingView username
  • Payment email (PayPal or crypto wallet)
  • Purchase date (if you have it)

Step 2: Confirmation Process

We'll confirm your refund request within 24-48 hours (usually faster). No lengthy justification needed — we trust you.

Step 3: Access Revocation

Once confirmed, your indicator access will be immediately revoked to prevent abuse.

Step 4: Refund Processing

Refunds are issued via your original payment method:

  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Cryptocurrency: 5-7 business days (manual processing)

Note: Cryptocurrency refunds are subject to network fees and market volatility. We refund the USD equivalent at the time of the original purchase.

4. What Happens After 7 Days?

4.1 No Refunds (Cancellation Available)

After 7 days, we do NOT offer refunds. However:

  • You can cancel your subscription anytime to prevent future charges
  • You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period
  • No partial refunds for unused time

4.2 Why No Refunds After 7 Days?

Our indicators are digital products with immediate access. The 7-day window gives you plenty of time to evaluate. After that, we assume you're satisfied and committed to using the service.

4.3 Cancellation Process

PayPal Subscriptions:

  • Accessible through PayPal → Settings → Payments → Manage automatic payments
  • Located under "Signal Pilot Labs" with cancellation option
  • Cancellation requests can be sent to support@signalpilot.io

Cryptocurrency Subscriptions:

  • Cancellation requests can be sent to support@signalpilot.io
  • No auto-renewal for crypto subscriptions

5. Special Circumstances

5.1 Technical Issues

Technical problems (indicators not loading, access issues, bugs) can be reported to support for troubleshooting and resolution before considering refunds.

Common issues we can fix:

  • Indicator not appearing in TradingView
  • Incorrect subscription tier activated
  • Payment processed but access not granted

If we can't resolve a legitimate technical issue, we may offer a refund outside the 7-day window at our discretion.

5.2 Billing Errors

Incorrect charges (duplicate charges, wrong amount, etc.) can be reported immediately for refunds or corrections regardless of the 7-day window.

5.3 Extenuating Circumstances

In rare cases (medical emergencies, serious financial hardship), refund requests outside the 7-day window may be considered on a case-by-case basis. Details can be sent to support@signalpilot.io.

6. Lifetime Access Refunds

⚠️ Important for Lifetime Buyers: Lifetime access is a one-time purchase with limited availability. After the 7-day window, NO refunds are provided. Treat this as a final purchase decision.

Because Lifetime Access is significantly discounted and capped at 100 licenses, the 7-day policy is strictly enforced. The trial period allows evaluation of whether Signal Pilot meets individual needs.

7. Subscription Auto-Renewal

7.1 How It Works

  • Monthly plans renew every 30 days
  • Quarterly plans renew every 90 days
  • Yearly plans renew every 365 days

7.2 Renewal Reminders

We'll email you 7 days before your subscription renews (if you've opted into emails). This gives you time to cancel if needed.

7.3 Renewal Refunds

If you forget to cancel and your subscription auto-renews:

  • Within 24 hours of renewal: We may issue a refund as a courtesy (first-time only)
  • After 24 hours: No refunds, but you can cancel to prevent the next renewal

Pro Tip: Set a calendar reminder a few days before renewal if you're unsure about continuing.

8. Chargebacks and Disputes

⚠️ Please contact us before filing a chargeback. Chargebacks hurt small businesses and trigger additional fees. We're happy to resolve any issues directly.

8.1 Our Approach

If you file a chargeback without contacting us first:

  • Your access is immediately revoked
  • Your account is permanently banned
  • We may dispute the chargeback and provide transaction records

8.2 Legitimate Disputes

If you believe you were charged fraudulently (stolen card, unauthorized transaction), file a chargeback immediately and notify us at support@signalpilot.io so we can investigate.
