## Chapter 4: The Scales

*"I weigh the invisible. Pressure does not lie."*

Price can rise on air. Price can fall on whispers. But pressure — cumulative, undeniable buying versus selling force — reveals the truth beneath the surface.

The Scales manifest as a ribbon of intention:
- **Green** — Demand conquers supply. Buyers control the battlefield.
- **Red** — Supply overwhelms demand. Sellers hold dominion.
- **White dots** — Extreme pressure reached. The tipping point approaches.

But The Scales' true power lies in *divergence* — when price moves one direction while pressure moves another. Hidden strength. Hidden weakness. The lie the candles tell, exposed.

When price makes new highs but Plutus Flow refuses to confirm, The Scales have spoken: *This throne is built on sand.*

*The fourth lesson of the void: Price can deceive. Pressure cannot.*
