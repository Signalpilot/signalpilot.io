MOMENTUM

Momentum ≠ Trend

Momentum is slope; trend is value migration. Treat them differently and your trades stop arguing with themselves.

Trend (bias): “Which side first?” Use EMA stacks, MACD+ regime, and higher‑timeframe anchors.
Momentum (impulse): “Is the move accelerating or fading?” Use RSI Triad / SRSI+ turns and Leg‑Timer squeezes.

Simple playbook

  • Trade with trend. Use momentum only to time entries and exits.
  • When momentum fades against the trend, look for first pullback, not reversal heroics.
  • When both trend and momentum align, size normally; when they disagree, size smaller or stand down.

Common mistakes

  • Shorting every overbought RSI while the regime is up.
  • Defining trend with an oscillator instead of value anchors and structure.
  • Assuming momentum reversal = trend reversal. Often it’s just a dip.
