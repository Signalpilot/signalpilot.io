Price vs. Value: Regime before Entries

First decide the regime—trend up/down or range. Then pick entries that agree. This prevents the most common mistake: fighting the tape.

Rule of thumb: trade with the regime. Counter‑trend is for later, with stricter rules.

Check regime in 60 seconds

  1. 1.
    HTF bias: Are EMA‑34/55 and price aligned? Is our MACD+ slope agreeing?
  2. 2.
    Anchors: Is price above/below dVWAP / wVWAP centers? Above supports, below supplies?
  3. 3.
    Structure: Last BOS/CHOCH? Are we expanding or reverting to a mean?

Only then: look for entries

  • With-trend: First pullback to demand/VWAP, PVA bull, RSI/SRSI turns.
  • Range: Fades at edges when flow dries; target the opposite edge, not glory.
  • Counter-trend: Need absorption + OBV div + EC Pro signal + HTF tolerance.

Why this works

Regime filters trash. It converts dozens of “maybe” signals into a handful of aligned ones, and it gives you permission to do nothing when the wind is against you.
